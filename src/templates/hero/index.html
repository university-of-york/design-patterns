Hero figures

Figures will stretch full-width when used within an o-wrapper--wide. The image takes up the full width of the screen, with the figure content centred on the screen, as if in a container with max-width of 1200px. Add a class of c-figure--full-width to the figure for this to work.

Captions move out to the very edges (top left, bottom right etc) of the image.

<figure class=" c-figure--hero">

  <div class="c-figure--hero-bg">
    <img src="https://unsplash.it/2400/1200/?image=128" />

    <div class="c-figure__content c-figure__content--bottom-centre c-figure__content--half c-figure__content--hero">
      <h3>Undergraduate study</h3>
      <p>Study at York and you'll graduate with more than a qualification.</p>
      <p><a href="#" class="c-btn c-btn--small">Find a course <i class="c-icon c-icon--search c-icon-after"></i> </a><a href="#" class="c-btn c-btn--small">Book an open day</a></p>
    </div>
  </div>
</figure>
